Can I Rightfully Complain About My Bus?

I scraped real-time bus arrivals at my local bus stop from 6am-9pm for a week, then interpolated the real-time arrivals from the scraped data. This is a visualization of the results.

VTA 'Next Network' Interactive Diagram

Over summer 2018, I put together a stylized, 45-degree diagram of the planned future frequent transit service in Silicon Valley. I then wrapped it in d3.js, allowing for elegant zooming and panning. Added features such as clicking on a route to zoom to its extent.

Future uses: Simplified transit navigation platforms. Intuitive illustration of planned networks in a variety of settings. Interactive transit information/wayfinding kiosks.

link
STM Frequent Transit Diagram

Rethought the STM's metro map. Broke tradition by changing north to 'Montreal north', allowing for a 90-degree alignment of metro lines. Restyled map symbols, ensured no station labels overlapped lines, added the frequent bus network to better convey frequent transit availability to users.
